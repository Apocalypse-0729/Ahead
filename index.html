    What We Do

    Research and Analysis: We conduct comprehensive studies on a wide range of topics, including health system responsiveness, pharma, gender disparities in health, elderly health, maternal health, nutrition, coverage of health insurance, IT, and its impact on health, comorbidity, and polymorbidity. Our research is published in leading peer-reviewed journals and presented at major conferences worldwide.

    Policy Advisory: Our experts provide evidence-based recommendations to government agencies, international organizations, and non-profits. We help shape policies that enhance health system efficiency, equity, and sustainability.

    Education and Training: We offer NPTEL courses, workshops, and seminars to educate students and interested researchers from various eminent institutions. Our programs are designed to equip participants with the skills and knowledge needed to address complex health and economic issues.

    Collaborative Projects: We partner with other health economists working in academic institutions and other research organizations, such as the Indian Council of Social Science Research and the Ministry of Education, Government of India, to tackle interdisciplinary health challenges. We leverage diverse perspectives and expertise through collaboration to achieve more comprehensive solutions.
